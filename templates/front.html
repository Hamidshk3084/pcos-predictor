About PCOS and Our Prediction Model

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a common hormonal disorder affecting many individuals worldwide. It can lead to irregular periods, weight gain, and fertility issues. Our AI-powered prediction model helps assess the risk of PCOS based on user inputs, providing valuable insights for early diagnosis and management.

We are a research project developed as part of a college initiative, utilizing Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) models to predict the risk of Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) in users. Our goal is to explore the potential of AI in healthcare and raise awareness about PCOS. However, this tool is strictly for research purposes and should not be considered a medical diagnosis. For accurate evaluation and treatment, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.
